Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you may return the item within 30 days of receipt.

Return Process:
  1. Contact our customer service to initiate a return.
  2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging.
  3. Ship the item back to us with the provided return label.

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.
